Work Archives

This section contains my past works that I hold pride in. It also covers the manifestation of my work and efforts. These experiences challenged me and empowered my pride as an artistic individual.

School Publication Newspaper

Being involved in things like this was such an eye-opener. Entering the school publication during my senior year was indeed a fruitful moment. I get to learn how to layout texts and to create designs as I envisioned. I learned a lot from here which really contributes to the deigning skills I currently have. Unfortunately, I wasn't able to totally focus on this due to personal matters as well as academics. In entirety, it was fun.

Grand Insignia: Magazine

Another work would probably be the Project: Grand Insignia, a magazine publication that I, along with my grade 12 classmates, established. We made a magazine about life (in general) and that people should be able to take hold of their fate. The designer of that magazine was yours truly, me, which took 2 months complete. More details in Grand Insignia Facebook Page.
